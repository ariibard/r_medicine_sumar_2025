SUMAR Program

A Result-Based Health Policy

SUMAR is a national policy in Argentina aimed at improving financing for the uninsured population through a result-based funding model.

Financed with World Bank support, it transfers funds to provinces based on:

  1. Identifying people with exclusive public coverage
  2. Providing essential health services (Basic Effective Coverage – CEB)
  3. Achieving health outcomes tracked by tracer indicators

Infrastructure and Automation in Buenos Aires City

Our Rol in GOGIES

In Buenos Aires City, our team plays a central role in automating SUMAR processes.

We focus on:

  • Identifying patients with priority conditions
  • Verifying services through the Health Information System (HIS)

Our main outputs:

  • PDF invoices (“cuasifacturas”)

  • Weekly Excel reports - Performance indicator summaries

The process involves three key stages:

  1. Enrollment
  2. Detection of Basic Effective Coverage services
  3. Analysis of performance indicators
